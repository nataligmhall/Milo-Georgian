Autobiography — Goderdzi Chokhelishvili

B1 — Intermediate · 28 words

After this lesson you can…

  • Use imperfect past: იბადებოდა, მიდიოდა
  • Talk about someone's life journey
  • Narrate events in sequence

Verb focus — იბადებოდა

to be born (imperfect) · Present — ibadeboda

PersonGeorgianEnglish
he/sheიბადებოდაwas being born
theyიბადებოდნენwere being born
IვიბადებოდიI was being born

Imperfect = ongoing/repeated past. Biography tense.

Grammar

Imperfect tense for past habits/narrative: იბადებოდა (was being born), მიდიოდა (was going), აღწევდა (was reaching). Biographical questions: როდის დაიბადე? სად?

  • Imperfect forms present ongoing or habitual past background.
  • Biographical narration often alternates dates and life events.
  • First-person storytelling can shift between factual and poetic tone.
  • Place-of-birth and family origin phrases are high-frequency.

💚 Easier than Russian: Georgian imperfect uses -დ- infix — one pattern, no aspect pairs like Russian.

Short reading

გოდერძი ჩოხელიშვილი ქართველი მწერალი იყო. იგი სოფელში იბადებოდა და თბილისში მიდიოდა სწავლისთვის. მისი ცხოვრება საინტერესო იყო. მკითხველები მას უყვარდათ.
Goderdzi Chokhelishvili was a Georgian writer. He was born in a village and went to Tbilisi to study. His life was interesting. Readers loved him.

Phrases & dialogues

🇷🇺 Моя семья часто переезжала.
