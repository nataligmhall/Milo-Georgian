📖 Grammar

Similative comparison: X-ის მსგავსი (similar to X), X-ივით (like X), ისეთი...როგორც (such...as). Advanced contracted stems continue in oblique forms.

  • Similarity can be expressed with -ის მსგავსი and -ივით.
  • Host-guest formulas are culturally fixed and widely reused.
  • Comparison structures help discuss traditions and manners.
  • Hospitality speech often blends respect and warmth markers.

🇷🇺 Сравнение по сходству: X-ის მსგავსი (похожий на X), X-ივით (как X), ისეთი...როგორც (такой...как). Продолжается тема сжатых основ в косвенных формах.

💚 Easier than Russian: Pattern -ივით is very direct for 'like/as', without separate case prepositions.
